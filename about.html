The Beginning

A Simple Idea That Changed Everything

In Woodbridge, Virginia, too many kids were watching from the sidelines — not because they didn't want to play, but because their families couldn't afford it.

Registration fees. Cleats. Uniforms. Shin guards. It all adds up. And for families already stretched thin, soccer becomes a luxury instead of a right.

TrueTouch United was created to fix that. We believe the beautiful game should be exactly that — beautiful, accessible, and free for every child who wants to play.
